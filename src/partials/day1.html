Day 1. Arrival. Meeting at the airport and accommodation

  • From a bird's eye view you will see the famous "home" volcanoes - Koryaksky, Avachinsky and Kozelsky. At the exit at the airport you will be met with a sign "DREAM TOUR".

  • On the way to the hotel, collecting funds from the members of the group, briefing on the program of this day (thermal water pool, barbecue, acquaintance with the group, alcohol, fireworks not earlier than 20:00).

  • After departure (the type of transport depends on the size of the group) to the recreation center in the resort village of Paratunka.

  • In the evening, after the arrival of all participants in Kamchatka (if the flights are different), a general meeting, checking equipment, discussing the program for tomorrow.
